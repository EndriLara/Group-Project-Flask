Read About Us


Three Codin Dojo academy students, Gentian Hoxha, Anxhela Fecanji, and Endri Lara, collaborated to develop a web application aimed at enhancing communication between renters.

The application simplifies the process of renting properties for both landlords and tenants, fostering a seamless interaction.

With a focus on user experience, the team strived to create an intuitive and efficient platform for users to easily find and rent properties that meet their needs.
